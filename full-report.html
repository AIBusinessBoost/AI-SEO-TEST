Key Findings

Technical SEO

Your website has several technical issues that are impacting search engine crawlability and indexation. These include slow page load times, missing meta descriptions, and improper header structure.

Content Quality

While your content is relevant to your industry, it lacks depth and keyword optimization. Many pages have thin content that doesn't fully address user search intent.

Backlink Profile

Your backlink profile is relatively weak compared to competitors. You have few high-authority domains linking to your site, limiting your domain authority.

Mobile Experience

Your website is not fully optimized for mobile devices, with issues in responsive design and touch elements that are too small or too close together.

Local SEO

Your local SEO presence needs improvement. Google Business Profile is incomplete, and local citations are inconsistent across directories.

AI Search Readiness

Your content is not optimized for AI search engines like ChatGPT. It lacks structured data and clear question-answer formats that AI systems prefer.

Priority Action Steps

  1. Improve page load speed by optimizing images, implementing browser caching, and minifying CSS/JavaScript files.
  2. Enhance meta descriptions and title tags for all pages with targeted keywords and compelling call-to-actions.
  3. Develop comprehensive content that thoroughly addresses user search intent and includes relevant keywords.
  4. Build quality backlinks through outreach to industry publications, guest posting, and creating shareable content.
  5. Optimize for mobile devices by implementing responsive design and ensuring touch elements are properly sized and spaced.
  6. Complete and verify your Google Business Profile and ensure consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information across all directories.
  7. Implement structured data markup using Schema.org to help search engines better understand your content.
  8. Format content for AI readability by using clear headings, question-answer formats, and concise, factual information.
